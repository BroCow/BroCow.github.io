Target Audience Description

The target audience consists of Idaho residents of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. These towns are mainly populated by younger families with a modest household income. A large part of the population consists of children under the age of 18 due to the amount of families that live in these towns. The average family size is around 3.5 people per household.

This demographic will likely be interested in how the weather will affect their outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, picnics, and local sporting events. They will also want to be informed of any weather that may impact school schedules. In the winter, residents of these towns will want to know what the road conditions are for travel as well.

Two Personas

The two personas are below:

  1. Family of Five

    2. This persona will be using the website to know how to dress their kids for school, how to get them where they need to go, and how the kids schedules may be impacted. This persona will also want to know what the weekend weather will be like so that outdoor family activities can be planned accordingly.

  2. Single Adult, 25 - 44 years old

    3. This persona will use the site to determine how to plan outdoor activities and travel. Most likely career based, this persona will need to know if the weather is going to affect their ability to do their job or get to work. Approximately 25% of the population in these towns fits into this persona.

Scenarios

Possible scenarios to consider are below:

  1. Is soccer practice going to be cancelled due to rain?
  2. Should the kids bring an umbrella and jacket to school?
  3. Will this weekend be a good option for a camping trip?
  4. Will the job site be closed due to foul weather and dangerous working conditions?
  5. Are there any road closures or poor traffic conditions due to the weather that will affect my commute?
