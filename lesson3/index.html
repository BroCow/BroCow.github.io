Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide up-to-date weather news and forecasts for specific locations, information about significant storms, and images of impressive weather.
Weather forecasts will be given for the following locations:

  • Preston
  • Soda Springs
  • Fish Haven

A page titled Storm Center will provide information about significant storms, and a page titled Gallery will make impressive weather images available.

The website will be focused on the residents of the three towns listed above and will focus on weather news and events affecting those areas.
