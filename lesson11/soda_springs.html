A Captive Geyser

By Sam Spraywater

Soda Springs is home to the only captive geyser in the world. In the 1930's, drilling was being done for a hot water source, but instead they discovered carbonated water that sprayed a spout over 100 feet into the air! The citizens capped it and now release it every hour for a truly amazing sight.

Come to Soda Springs for this and more! This city is full of history, fun, and beauty. You are sure to have a great time as you get to know the city and what it has to offer!
