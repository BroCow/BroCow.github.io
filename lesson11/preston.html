Outdoor Family Fun

By Hank Hikealot

Nothing is more exhilarating than a hike surrounded by the beauty of nature. Here in Preston, you can find trails that are sure to provide you with what you are looking for. From easy, Stop and smell the flowers hikes to, Test your endurance hikes, Preston has it.

In addition to great scenery and exercise, a hiker in Preston will also be pleased to discover a wide variety of wild life. There are many species of birds and mammals that present fantastic photo opportunities. You will truly feel like you are one with nature while out on your hike!
